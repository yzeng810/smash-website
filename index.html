2020 Democratic Presidential Candidates

A lot of candidates have decided to run for president of the United States in the upcoming 2020 elections. I wanted to focus on three notable U.S. senators (Elizabeth Warren, Kirsten Gillibrand, adn Kamala Harris) who have decided to run and recently announced their candidacy and/or intention to run. An interesting thing to note that only one of these women, Senator Kamala Harris, has officially announced her campaign. Senators Kirsten Gillibrand and Elizabeth Warren have formed exploratory committees and should make an official announcement soon. All three women are older than 50 years old, are U.S. senators from relatively large and well-known states (Massachusetts, New York, and California), and are heavily experienced with public service. I've ordered the senators by date of candidacy announcement, as Elizabeth Warren announced her candidacy on 12/31/2018, Kirsten Gillibrand announced hers on 01/15/2019, and Kamala Harris announced hers on 01/21/2019. Classical Pianists

Who are the greatest pianists who've ever lived? That's the question we put to the Classic FM presenters and – after much heated debate – these are my most favorite artists. Mozart could be considered the first "concert pianist" as he performed widely on the piano. Composers Beethoven and Clementi from the classical era were also famed for their playing, as were, from the romantic era, Liszt, Brahms, Chopin, Mendelssohn and Rachmaninoff. From that era, leading performers less known as composers were Clara Schumann and Hans von Bülow. However, as we do not have modern audio recordings of most of these pianists, we rely mainly on written commentary to give us an account of their technique and style.

  • Sergei Rachmaninov
  • Glenn Gould

Famous Religious Figures

Religion and spirituality are two things that are probably the most difficult to define in definite terms. It would be wrong to say that spirituality and religion don’t go hand in hand because they do; the only difference is that while religion is an organized system that pays homage to a god, spirituality is something that does not always need a deity to define the parameters of the person’s spiritual conduct. Throughout history there have been people who have played the roles of, both, spiritual and religious leaders for their societies. Some of them have done such exemplary work, with such dedication, that their work, and in turn they themselves, were recognized the world over for their societies.
